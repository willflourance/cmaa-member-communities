The Grapevine

The Grapevine is the Wine Society’s quarterly newsletter. Each edition features chapter and member news; Society happenings; and spotlight articles of interest for wine lovers and aficionados including wineries, travel, sales and service and trends.

To submit an item for consideration for inclusion, please contact Editor, Melissa Low

Current Grapevine - Winter 2019

From the Host: Cheers to 2019!

Old man winter finally arrived, and while I live in a state that maintains sunshine 350 days a year, I still choose to diversify what’s in my glass to match the season. Like you, I am a creature of habit that gradually changes beverage preferences every few years. This year, I find myself still on barrel-aged cocktails while enjoying wines from northern Italy and Rhone with the traditional braised winter dishes and heartier fare.

Jason Asbra, Host
