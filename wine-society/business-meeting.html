About

At the World Conference on Club Management and Club Business Expo, the International Wine Society holds its Annual Business Meeting and Education Session. At the Annual Business Meeting, the Board of Managers is elected and business pertaining to the Society is discussed. Immediately following the Business Meeting is the Education Session, where an industry expert discusses various aspects of wine and then conducts a tasting. A wine test is also given at this meeting with the five highest scores participating in a blind tasting. The winner of the blind tasting is awarded a plaque and designs the test for the following year. Attendees are awarded credits for their attendance to the Wine Society Business Meeting.
