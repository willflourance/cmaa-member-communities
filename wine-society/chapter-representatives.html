Chapter Representatives

About

The Wine Society Representative shall:
  • Serve as the liaison between the chapter and the International Wine Society;
  • Assist the chapter in furthering their wine education;
  • Plan and implement one wine-related education program each year;
  • Recruit interested members for the Wine Society;
  • Organize the chapter’s drive to collect wine and wine-related items for the Wine Society’s Annual Wine Auctions; and
  • Manage wine education for the chapter.
  • Hold the position for at least seven months.

The Wine Society Chapter Representative must be a current IWS Member. The Wine Society Representative will report directly to their supervising officer on the Wine Society Board of Managers. The fulfillment of responsibilities will be verified by the Chapter President.

*Association Activity Credits are granted for this position.
