The 2019 Wine Auctions will be held in conjunction with the CMAA 2019 World Conference and Club Business Expo, February 24-28, 2019.

The auctions are a success thanks to generosity of our club managers and chapters. Donations to the wine auctions have sparked a healthy competition among the chapters to donate the most wine. CMAA chapters compete for one of the two coveted traveling Bucky trophies.

In an effort to streamline the auction process, the Club Foundation and Annual Wine Auctions have moved from paper and pencil to an online platform and we have employed the experts at GiveSmart, a silent auction technology company. Bidders can preregister now for the auction by visiting the Auction Website or in person with the swipe of their credit card.

Bidders will participate from their smartphone to place bids and set max bids regardless of their location at the Conference or the time of day. Bids can also be placed by text message and there are on-site mobile kiosks in the auction area.

Checkout is made easy, as you can pay your bill as soon as the auction closes. Just don’t forget to pick-up your winning lots on Thursday, February 28.

Silent Auctions – Bidding will run from Monday, February 25 at 10:00 a.m. through Wednesday, February 27 at 3:40 p.m. Bidders may view the merchandise in in Member Services Pavilion (location TBD), during the following dates and times:

Monday, February 25 10:00 a.m. - 5:30 p.m.

Tuesday, February 26 7:30 a.m. - 5:30 p.m.

Wednesday, February 27 7:30 a.m. - 5:00 p.m.
