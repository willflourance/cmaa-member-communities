Wine Auction Donations

We will start accepting donations for the 2018 Auction on January 1, 2019.

Auction Donation Form →

2019 Auction Shipping Reimbursement Form →

The wine auctions have sparked a healthy competition among the chapters to donate the most wine. CMAA chapters compete for one of the two coveted traveling Bucky trophies→

To make a donation, please fill out the Auction Donation Form and include the confirmation page with each box you ship:

IWS Auction Donation
Attn: Wes Hardin
Belle Meade Country Club
815 Belle Meade Blvd.
Nashville, TN 37205

Belle Meade Country Club will start accepting donation deliveries AFTER January 1, 2019. All donations should be shipped by Friday, February 15, 2019 and have the value of at least $25 per bottle and $500 in wholesale value to be eligible for reimbursement by The Club Foundation.

Requests for reimbursement must be submitted no later than Monday, March 25, 2019, to CMAA National Headquarters by one individual from each chapter. Please use the Auction Shipping Reimbursement Form to submit your request with a receipt for all shipping and supply charges.

Onsite donations will be accepted up until Saturday, February 23, 2019, 10:00 a.m. and must be dropped off in the auction area of the Member Services Pavilion. On-site donations must be submitted via the Auction Donation Form prior to being dropped-off. The online Donation Form helps the Wine Society determine the Bucky winner annually. Failure to complete and include the Auction Donation Form with your donation may prevent your chapter from winning the Bucky Award.

The Wine Auctions are a success thanks to generosity of our club managers and chapters!
