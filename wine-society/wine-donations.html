Wine Auction Donations

We are now accepting auction donations.

Auction Donation Form →

2020 Auction Shipping Reimbursement Form →

The wine auctions have sparked a healthy competition among the chapters to donate the most wine. CMAA chapters compete for one of the two coveted traveling Bucky trophies→

To make a donation, please fill out the Auction Donation Form and include the confirmation page with each box you ship:

IWS Auction Donation
Attn: Raymond DeTullio
Lakewood Country Club
6430 Gaston Ave.
Dallas, TX 75214

Lakewood Country Club is now accepting donation.  All donations should be shipped by Thursday, January 30, 2020 and have the value of at least $25 per bottle and $500 in wholesale value to be eligible for reimbursement by The Club Foundation.

Requests for reimbursement must be submitted no later than Monday, March 6, 2020, to CMAA National Headquarters by one individual from each chapter. Please use the Auction Shipping Reimbursement Form to submit your request with a receipt for all shipping and supply charges.

Onsite donations will be accepted up until Friday, February 7, 2020, 10:00 a.m. and must be dropped off in the Wine Auction Room (location TBD). On-site donations must be submitted via the Auction Donation Form prior to being dropped-off. The online Donation Form helps the Wine Society determine the Bucky winner annually. Failure to complete and include the Auction Donation Form with your donation may prevent your chapter from winning the Bucky Award.

The Wine Auctions are a success thanks to generosity of our club managers and chapters!
