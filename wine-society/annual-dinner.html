Annual Dinner
International Wine Society Annual Wine Dinner

Each year at CMAA’s World Conference and Club Business Expo, the International Wine Society holds an annual gourmet wine dinner. This event is an extraordinary success each year and tickets sell out quickly.

The annual dinner normally features a guest speaker from a major winery who moderates the event while participants are tantalized with delectable dishes served with the vineyard’s recommended wines to complement each course.

This year’s dinner will be held at the Nelson’s Green Briar Distillery in Nashville, Tennessee on Wednesday, February 27, 2019. Join us for an evening of companionship, education and appreciation for fine food and wine.

Admission

In order to simplify the process for the Wine Society's Annual Dinner, tickets are required upon entrance to the dinner. Members who purchase tickets for the event will receive them in advance of the dinner.

If necessary, Wine Society members who purchase tickets after the event has been filled will be placed on a waiting list upon receipt of payment by CMAA National Headquarters. If any cancellations occur, the event will be filled by Wine Society Members on the waiting list (in the order in which the payments were received).

For more information contact Sara Thom or call (703) 739-9500.
