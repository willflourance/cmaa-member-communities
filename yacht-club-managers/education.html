Education

Connect and network with other yacht club management professionals on Monday, February 25. These education sessions are specifically curated and facilitated for managers of yacht clubs. All sessions will take place at the Music City Center.

Registration for this slate of education is separate from general Conference registration, and covers the cost of lunch.

Schedule

  • 10:15 – 11:45 a.m.

    Review of Yacht Club Trends and Statistics from Annual Survey

    Ray Cronin, Club Benchmarking

    During this program, the results from the most recent survey of yacht club managers, conducted by Club Benchmarking will be reviewed and discussed.

  • 11:45 a.m. – 12:45 p.m.

    Lunch

  • 1:00 – 2:00 p.m.

    Focusing on the Horizon: US Sailing’s Strategic Plan 2018-2022

    Patrick Meade, US Sailing

    Patrick Meade, the Engagement Coordinator for the US Sailing Organization, will present US Sailing’s Strategic Plan for 2018-2022 with a special focus on the role of sailing organizations in its vision for the future. This will be followed by a discussion on best practices for increasing sailing participation at yacht clubs of all sizes.

  • 2:15 – 3:45 p.m.

    Yacht Club Panel and Roundtable Discussion

    Terry J. Anglin, CCM, CCE, San Diego Yacht Club and John R. “Jack” Sullivan, CCM, Kopplin, Kuebler & Wallace

    This program gives yacht club managers a chance to hear about challenges that yacht clubs face along with exchanging ideas on all areas of yacht club operations. Discover the best practices that your peers are using successfully every day in their facilities.
