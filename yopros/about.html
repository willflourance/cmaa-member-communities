About

Our Mission

CMAA's Young Professionals (YoPros) is a group dedicated to connecting and addressing the issues faced by young club management professionals, aged 40 and under. Engagement in this demographic group is largely from former CMAA Student members who advance to Alumnus or Professional membership. There is currently no application process to become involved in this member community.

Our History

Along with the support and facilitation of the CMAA staff, YoPros are steered by an advisory board of about 10 active managers. Some chapters have very active YoPro groups.

The YoPros host annual events and activities (at the CMAA World Conference), such as:

  • Education Panels/Roundtables
  • Student Education Session and Mentor Luncheon
  • Networking Mixer
  • Community Outreach
