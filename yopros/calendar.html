Mark Your Calendars for Upcoming YoPro Events!

Young Professionals Networking Event at the CMAA 2019 World Conference and Club Business Expo

Monday, February 25, 6:00 – 8:30 p.m.

Join your other 40 and under friends for a fun evening of problem solving at The Escape Game! Participants will split into groups for one hour each to strategize their way through rooms via a series of puzzles and riddles, using various clues and hints. After your game, check out one of the local restaurants with your new-found friends for a bite to eat!

Tickets for this event can be purchased via the CMAA World Conference registration form. Space for this event is limited and may not be available for purchase onsite.

Service Event and School Supply Drive for PENCIL, Presented by the CMAA YoPros

Tuesday, February 26, 9:30 a.m. – 3:30 p.m.
Wednesday, February 27, 9:30 a.m. – 1:00 p.m.

CMAA YoPros will be collecting donations of school supplies for PENCIL, a local organization that provides community resources to Metro Nashville Public Schools. The supplies collected during this drive will then be made available to local teachers for use in their classes, at no cost to them, allowing them to meet the educational needs of their students. While this initiative is sponsored by the CMAA YoPros, all Conference attendees are asked to consider donating to this cause. Collection bins will be set up in the Club Business Expo hall and near the Member Services Pavilion during the hours listed.

If you would like to make a monetary donation in lieu of school supplies to PENCIL, you can do so online at: https://give.classy.org/cmaapencil. PENCIL is a 501(c)3 tax-exempt organization and your donation is tax-deductible within the guidelines of U.S. law. To claim a donation as a deduction on your U.S. taxes, please keep your email donation receipt as your official record that will be sent to you upon successful completion of your donation.
